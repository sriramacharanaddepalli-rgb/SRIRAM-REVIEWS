# 🎬 Sriram Reviews | Professional Cinema Archive

A high-performance cinematic hub for film analysis, industry news, and multimedia management. Curated by **Sriram**, a Professional Review Writer.

## 👤 Identity
This platform serves as a digital portfolio for Sriram, focusing on:
* **In-Depth Reviews:** Technical breakdowns of Tollywood screenplay and directorial structure.
* **Movie News:** A standalone, real-time feed for industry updates and trending cinema news.
* **Media Curation:** Organized archives for movie posters and video reviews.

## 🚀 System Features
1. **Universal Deep Search:** A high-speed search bar that filters Title, Content, and News categories simultaneously.
2. **Dedicated Movie News Page:** Industry updates are separated from cinematic reviews for better navigation.
3. **Writing Studio:** A full-screen rich-text editor for professional scriptwriting and analysis.
4. **Cinematic UI:** A spotlight-driven design with professional "SR" initial branding.

## ⚙️ Usage
* **Navigation:** Toggle between Archive, Reviews, and Movie News using the navbar.
* **Adding Content:** Use the **(+)** button on any page to open the Studio.
* **Data Persistence:** All content is saved locally to the browser's storage.
